About us

Healing Touch, Magic Hands, Miracle Man are some of the names Aashish Kusswaha is called by his clients & fans in the sports industry. He has helped many elite sports enthusiasts to become Champion Medal winners for their countries in major world events. After working as a Chief Sports Therapist for more than 10 years with wide range of therapy methods and techniques, Aashish founded the TWO well-known sports therapy centres of Bengaluru - International Medical & Sports Massage Therapy Clinic (IMSMTC) AND Wellness Sports Inc.

Aashish’s vision is to make this unique preventive & recovery therapy available to every common man and add value to every aspiring young or old, fresh or matured sportsperson in the country. To fulfil his vision, Aashish passionately trains a bunch of male & female sportspersons who have unfortunately have to abandon their on-field dream of chasing medals at the national & international level to become someone who treat those who get many more medals for the country through two of his therapy centres.

Further, to make these treatments & therapies available & affordable, Aashish’s centres are located at Nagarabhavi (near Sports Authority of India) and Kanteerava Stadium, the hub of sports activities in Bengaluru and priced starting at less than a thousand rupees with special prices for those who represent our country as a mark of love & respect to Indian Sports.

Aashish & his team has served more than 8,000 sports personalities and delivered more than 1,45,000 treatments and the numbers are growing rapidly every single hour. With the high ability to relate well with the staff and the clients alike & work well in high-end, challenging environments, Aashish & team offers varieties of therapy massages and treatments. Therapies include Chiropractic Therapy, Sports Massage Therapy, Tung Acupuncture & Holographic Gua Sha Therapy. List of Massages include - Sports Massage, Medical Massage, Deep Tissue work, Fascia Massage, Hot Stone Massage, Acupuncture Therapy, Cupping Therapy, Reflexology. The clients treated by him are with injuries ranging from acute muscle strains to complex, chronic condition where the clients are immobile/bed-ridden.

List of key clients include - Indian Swimming team, Indian Hockey team, National Cricket academy, Indian Badminton team, Premier Badminton league, Long distance Running, Football Tata Mumbai Marathon, TCS World 10K, Indian Para athletes and Celebrities of Kannada Film Industry. Among very few in India to provide treatment by the method of Tung Acupuncture, Aashish uses his expertise in Functional Anatomy, Exercise, Exercise Physiology, Evaluation and Treatment of Myofascial Disorders, Specialized Soft Tissue Techniques (e.g. Trigger Point Therapy) Manipulation & all the Chronic disease treatments.
